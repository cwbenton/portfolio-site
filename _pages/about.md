My name is Connor Benton, and I am a statistician and data scientist in the Atlanta area. I have an MS in Data Science and Analytics and a BS in Mathematics from Kennesaw State University. My academic and professional interests lie in Bayesian and non-parametric statistical methods in medicine and American politics.

As a statistician, I enjoy tackling challenging problems and devising solutions mathematically. Currently, I intern at the Federal Bureau of Investigation in Atlanta, where I leverage data to assist with casework. Additionally, I lecture on mathematics, data science, and statistics, hoping to share my passion for these fields with others. Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy running, reading, playing the piano, and homelabbing! You can explore some of my projects and contributions on my page.
